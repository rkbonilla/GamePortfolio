About Me

Hello! My name is Roberto and I like to create educational games. I've enjoyed playing video games my entire life, and when was younger I played many educational games that helped teach me Math, English, and Geography, so I decided to make development of those games my career!

Currently, I am working on a Bachelor's in Game Development and Simulation Programming at the New England Institute of Technology. I have achieved at least a
3.9 GPA in all completed semesters, and am expected to graduate in March of 2024.

I also work for the National Elevator Industry making simulations and virtual reality games as part of our curriculum for current elevator mechanic apprentices. Students use these projects to as part of their 4 year apprenticeship as a supplement to on the job training.
